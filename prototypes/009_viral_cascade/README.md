## Gameplay

### Core Mechanic

"Inject viruses into a 6×6 grid, watch them spread via cellular automata, then harvest connected clusters for massive chain damage."

The game revolves around a single tactical loop:
1. **DRAW**: Receive 3 virus cards (RED, BLUE, GREEN; GOLD from turn 3)
2. **INJECT**: Place viruses onto empty grid cells
3. **SPREAD**: Viruses propagate to adjacent empty cells (2 generations, 55% chance each)
4. **HARVEST**: Choose a color — all connected clusters of that color detonate
5. **ENEMY ATTACKS**: Boss scales with turn count and heat level

### Virus Types

| Virus | Color | Multiplier | Special Effect |
|-------|-------|-----------|----------------|
| RED   | Red   | ×3 damage | Pure damage |
| BLUE  | Blue  | ×1 damage | +1 Shield per cell |
| GREEN | Green | ×1 damage | +1 Heal per cell |
| GOLD  | Gold  | ×5 damage | Costs 1 HP per cell (unlocked turn 3) |

### Risk System

- **Overflow**: Clusters larger than 5 cells cause self-damage (1 HP per excess cell)
- **Heat**: Total viruses on board = heat level. Heat > 15 causes overheat damage
- **Scaling**: Enemy attack increases with turns and excess heat

## Controls

| Action | Input |
