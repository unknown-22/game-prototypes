## Gameplay

### Core Mechanic

The paddle cycles through **4 element colors** (FIRE/RED, WATER/BLUE, EARTH/GREEN, LIGHT/YELLOW). The ball only **damages bricks of the matching color**. Non-matching bricks deflect the ball harmlessly but **reset your combo**.

### Combo → SUPER BALL

Breaking same-color bricks consecutively builds the **COMBO** meter. At **6+ combo**, the next paddle hit launches a **SUPER BALL** (glowing white) that **pierces through all brick colors** for massive chain damage. After the super ball returns to the paddle, combo resets.

### Risk/Reward

- **Stay on one color** to build combo toward SUPER BALL
- **Switch colors** to handle threatening brick columns, but lose your combo
- **Power-ups** drop from destroyed bricks — collect them before they fall off screen

### Waves

6 predefined waves with increasing rows, brick HP, and ball speed. Clear all bricks to advance. After wave 6, loops at max difficulty with score bonus.

## Controls

| Input | Action |
|---|---|
| **Mouse move** | Move paddle |
| **Left click / Space** | Launch ball (aim phase), restart (game over) |
| **Q / Scroll Down** | Previous color |
| **E / Scroll Up** | Next color |
| **1-4 keys** | Jump to specific color |
| **R** | Restart any time |
